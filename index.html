About Me

Dr. P.P. Shajimon joined St. Thomas College Of Teacher Education, Pala on 16th March 2006 as Assistant Professor in Social Science Education. He has more than 28 years of teaching experience in various Colleges of Teacher Education, under Mahatma Gandhi University. He qualified B.A (Economics) from St. Thomas College, Pala; M.A (Economics) from Nirmala College, Muvattupuzha,M.A.(Sociology) from IGNOU, Delhi, B.Ed (History) from University College of Teacher Education,Adoor, Kerala University, M.Ed (Social Science) from School of Pedagogical Sciences, Mahatma Gandhi University and Ph.D from Mahatma Gandhi University. He also qualified NET (Education). His interesting areas of teaching are Social Science Education, Philosophy of Education, Sociology of Education, Indian Philosophy, Elementary Education, Secondary Education, Teacher Education and Guidance and Counselling.

I was a Research Guide at Bharathiar University, Coimbatore, Tamil Nadu. I supervised many Ph.D Theses of Bharathiar University and M.Ed Dissertations of Mahatma Gandhi University, Kottayam. I've completed two Minor Research Projects and published many research papers in reputed journals. I had co-ordinated seminars, participated and presented many papers in various seminars at national and international levels.
